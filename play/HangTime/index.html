Hang with friends.HangTime is a scheduling app for students that is on a mission to make your student life more social, convenient, and productive.

Social Integration

HangTime links with your Facebook account so you can find your friends on HangTime and request for their schedules easily.

Real-Time Sync

HangTime provides its users with cutting-edge real-time updates on recent changes to schedules made by you or your friends. This lets you keep track and conveniently plan out hangouts and meetings throughout the week.


Material Design

HangTime uses an intuitive interface that follows the principles of Material Design, the design language used by Google and many other top companies.

SMS Notifications

HangTime integrates with an SMS-sending API to send you personalized texts about requests for meetings, updates about your friends, and upcoming events happening at the times you are free.
