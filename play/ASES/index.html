The hottest student entrepreneurship summit in Asia is back.

Every year, the Asia-Pacific Student Entrepreneurship Society (ASES) organizes one Stanford Summit in the spring and one Asia Summit at one of the ASES international chapters in the summer.

This year, ASES Philippines is proud to organize this year's summit again - ASES Summit Manila 2016. This year's summit will be the biggest, hottest, and best Summit in Manila yet.

Major Details

Theme: Social Technopreneurship - Using Technology to Create Scalable Impact in Emerging Markets No. of Delegates: 40 Target Date: July 25-31, 2016 Earlybird Deadline and Pricing: $350 | Regular Deadline and Pricing: $400

Major Events

Day 1-2: GK ENCHANTED FARM

The GK Enchanted Farm experience will offer a tour of the different social enterprises and tourist spots in the farm, talks from prominent Filipino social entrepreneurs, and a cultural night to give the delegates an opportunity to share about their country's culture. On the second day, teams will be given the opportunity to work on their idea. Dinner will be a bonding experience over a food trip in Manila.


Day 3: SOCIAL TECHNOPRENEURSHIP IN ATENEO DE MANILA UNIVERSITY

Talks about social technopreneurship from some of the best-known people in the industry will be held. At night, as a hustling challenge, teams will be given money and shopping lists, and the team that can buy everything for the least amount of money will win.


Day 4: BEACH TRIP WITH DESIGN AND PROTOTYPING WORKSHOPS

Workshops on prototyping, material design, and branding will be given by design experts on the beach. This will give the teams enough time to develop their idea and prototypes.


Day 5: STARTUP CRAWL AND WORKING TIME

Delegates will be brought to Subic Bay for a day at the beach. Workshops on prototyping, material design, and branding will be given by design experts and professionals. Teams will have to think of their users and how to design their product to appeal to their prospective customers. This gives the teams enough time to develop their idea and already start making sample mock-ups/prototypes of their product.


Day 6: COMPETITION PROPER AT THE UNIVERSITY OF THE PHILIPPINES

The teams will give pitch their ideas to a panel of judges. After the winners are announced, a farewell dinner will be held, followed by a night to let loose at the premier clubbing scene in the Metro.
