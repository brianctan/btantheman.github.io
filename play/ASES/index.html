It's back.

The hottest student entrepreneurship summit in Asia is back.

Every year, the Asia-Pacific Student Entrepreneurship Society (ASES) organizes one Stanford Summit in the spring and one Asia Summit at one of the ASES international chapters in the summer.

This year, ASES Philippines is proud to organize this year's summit again - ASES Summit Manila 2016. This year's summit will be the biggest, hottest, and best Summit in Manila yet.


What's In Store?

Meet Inspiring Speakers
Get the chance to hear from and ask questions to the Philippines' best social entrepreneurs and start up founders.
Build a Global Network
Meet a diverse selection of student delegates from around the world passionate about making change through entrepreneurship.
Solve Real Problems
The Summit includes a pitching competition that will challenge you to think of innovative solutions to real social problems.

Major Events

Day 1-2: GK ENCHANTED FARM

The GK Enchanted Farm experience will offer you a tour of the different social enterprises and tourist spots in the farm, talks from prominent Filipino social entrepreneurs, and a cultural night to let the delegates share about their country's culture. On the second day, teams will be given the opportunity to work on their idea for the competition.


Day 3: TALKS AT ATENEO DE MANILA UNIVERSITY

On Day 3, listen to talks from some of the best-known entrepreneurs in the local startup scene. At night, get ready for a fun adventure while being able to do market validation for your idea.


Day 4: BEACH TRIP WITH DESIGN AND PROTOTYPING WORKSHOPS

On Day 4, we travel to the beach for workshops on design thinking and rapid prototyping given by design experts. This will give the teams enough time to develop their idea and prototypes.


Day 5: STARTUP CRAWL AND WORKING TIME

On Day 5, get the chance to tour the offices of some of the best tech companies here in the Philippines. In the afternoon, have time to get feedback from mentors and work on your pitch late into the night.


Day 6: COMPETITION PROPER AT THE UNIVERSITY OF THE PHILIPPINES

On the last day, teams will do their pitch to a panel of esteemed judges and investors. After the winners are announced, a farewell dinner will be held, followed by a night to let loose at a premier club in the Metro.
